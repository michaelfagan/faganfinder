Introduction

YouTube may be the most popular website for videos, but there are a lot of videos that you won’t find there.

You can use this page to run the same search across a wide range of sites for finding movies, television, and other videos as well as reviews, information, and more.

This page emphasizes free resources, however some of the videos which can be found are restricted, i.e. they may require free registration, not be available in all regions, or require a fee or paid subscription.

Search engines

Google, Bing, Yandex, blinkx, and MeFeedia are all search engines which can find videos from many different hosts, including those on this page. Most results are free to watch, but Bing and Google include some paid videos.

Social media

Use the Facebook and Twitter searches above to find videos shared on those social sites. You must be logged into Facebook to get proper search results from it.

Vine was a social media site exclusively for sharing 6-second looping videos. Although closed to new videos, existing ones can still be found.

Video hosts

Most websites do not host their own videos, but instead host them on YouTube, DailyMotion, Vimeo, Rumble, Veoh, Metacafe, and other popular video hosting sites. This is often cheaper, enables greater distribution, and includes useful features.

While the vides hosted on these sites are generally included in search engines, some can be missed, and the host sites often have additional features not available from the search engines.

Long videos

These are the most popular video search tools, filtered to find videos with a duration of 20 minutes or longer.

Movies and series

These websites include only professionally-made movies, television shows, and web series.

  • Tubi TV has a large library of movies and TV shows including major Hollywood studios, anime, etc. and is not available in all countries.
  • Yahoo! View includes content from major American film and television companies, anime, etc., and can only be viewed in the United States.
  • Popcornflix includes mostly independent films and can only be viewed in the United States and Canada.
  • Viewster includes international and independent films, documentaries, anime, and other movies and TV shows.
  • AOL Video includes mostly original web series.

Although they can’t be searched from this page, see also:

  • Crackle, which includes Hollywood, anime, original web series, and other movies and TV shows, and is not available in all countries.
  • SnagFilms, which includes mostly independent film and TV shows and documentaries.
