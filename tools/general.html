While Google is the most popular search engine, it is not the only one. Each search engine has its own:

  • database of web pages, refreshed on different dates
  • method of ranking web pages
  • operators and filters
  • extra features
  • results display

Because of this, any one of them might provide the best results or answer to what you are looking for. Bing (by Microsoft) is the second most popular search engine, and Yandex is the most popular in Russia. Exalead and Gigablast are the remaining major ones. Mojeek

Advanced searching

It can be very helpful to learn the keywords and special characters that search engines understand. They each have their own, but all support using quotation marks around a phrase to find those exact words together, and putting a minus symbol before a word to exclude it. For more, see search operators for:

  • Google (not all are listed on their own website, so see a more comprehensive list of Google search operators)
  • Bing
  • Yandex
  • Exalead
  • Gigablast

These are tools which do not have their own database of web pages to search. Instead, they use others’ while providing different features.

All of these happen to have privacy as a primary feature. Other than DuckDuckGo, they are all based in Europe. They also show the same results to everyone, whereas Google, Yandex, and sometimes Bing personalize their results.

DuckDuckGo features include instant answers and bangs. It combines results from Bing and Yandex. See DuckDuckGo search help.
